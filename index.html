PacBecca

Rules and leaderboard

Objective

Clear all pickups through 10 levels while avoiding Frosty, Megasen, Aspyn, Smeag, and Captain.

Rules

  • Pellets and hearts add score and fill Becca Burst.
  • Yellow cans make ghosts vulnerable.
  • After one yellow can, the first bad ghost hit triggers a 5-second hypno-rainbow save.
  • Space uses Becca Burst when the meter is full.
  • Clear every pickup to advance.
